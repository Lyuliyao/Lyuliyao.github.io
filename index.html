Biography [CV]

Embarking on a Ph.D. in Computational Mathematics, Science, and Engineering at Michigan State University in fall 2020, my academic background includes a Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Soochow University, obtained in July 2020. My research under Professor Jingrun Chen has focused on Computational Chemistry and Machine Learning. My educational path also features participation in the UC Berkeley Extension Program in Spring 2019 and a research internship at HKUST under Professor Yang Xiang during Summer 2019, enriching my research capabilities. Additionally, in Fall 2019, I expanded my expertise in computational methods through a visiting student position at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth, working on the Discontinuous Galerkin Method with Professor Zheng Chen.
