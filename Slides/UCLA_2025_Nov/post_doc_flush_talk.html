Measure Valued Neural Network

  • Instead of interested in the interactions between individuals like previous work.
  • We focus on the interactions between an individual and the distribution of the population.
  • Mckean type SDE: \[ \intd \mathbf X_t = F(\mathbf X_t, \mu_t) \intd t + G(\mathbf X_t, \mu_t) \intd \mathbf W_t. \]
  • New neural network architecture with measure valued inputs. \[ F(\mathbf x, \mu) \approx \varphi_\theta\left(\mathbf x, \int \psi_\theta(\mathbf y) \intd \mu(\mathbf y)\right). \]
  • Universial approximation theorem and scaling law for the new architecture.

Current Work: No mass conservation System

  • ODE or SDE description of the nonconservative system
  • For example:
  • phase separation - allen cahn equation \[ \partial_t u = \Delta u + u - u^3. \]
  • chemotactic - Keller-Segel model \[ \partial_t u = \Delta u - \chi \nabla \cdot (u \nabla v), \quad \partial_t v = D \Delta v + \alpha u - \beta v. \]
  • Approximate the solution \(u\) by the empirical measure of particles: \[ t \to \mu^N(t) = \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i \in \mathcal I(N,t)} \delta_{\mathbf X_i^N(t)}. \]
  • Advection and diffusion step: Each particle follows an SDE: \( dX_t = b(X_t)\,dt + \sigma\,dW_t \)
  • Reaction step: The reaction term \( r(u)u \) is handled by a stochastic birthâ€“death process:
    • For \( r(x)\!>\!0 \): particles branch with rate \( e^{r(x)\tau}-1 \).
    • For \( r(x)\!<\!0 \): particles die with survival probability \( e^{r(x)\tau} \).
  • Under the Global Lipschitz and Boundedness condition of the coefficients, we can prove the convergence of measure-valued process \( \mu^N \) to the solution of the target PDE with respect to \( H^{-s} \) norm for \( s > d/2 + 1 \).
Liyao Lyu, Huan Lei*. "A stochastic branching particle method for solving non-conservative reaction-diffusion equations." arXiv preprint arXiv:2510.27615 (2025).
